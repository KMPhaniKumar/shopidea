## 1. App Name Options

### Criteria for a Good Name
- Easy to say in Hindi and English
- Easy to spell and remember
- Short — ideally 1-2 words
- Available as .com domain
- Available on Play Store and App Store
- Reflects the idea — local sellers, social commerce, India

## 3. How to Register the Business in India

### Step 1 — Choose Business Structure

| Structure | Best For | Cost | Time |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Sole Proprietorship** | Solo founder, testing idea | ₹2,000-5,000 | 1 week |
| **Private Limited Company** | If raising investment, multiple founders | ₹10,000-15,000 | 2-3 weeks |
| **LLP (Limited Liability Partnership)** | 2+ founders, no investor plans | ₹5,000-8,000 | 2 weeks |

**Recommendation: Start as Sole Proprietorship to test, convert to Pvt Ltd before raising investment**

---

### Step 2 — Register as Sole Proprietorship (Simplest Route)

**Documents needed:**
- Aadhaar card
- PAN card
- Bank account in business name
- Address proof

**Process:**
```
1. Open a current account in your business name at any bank
   → Go to bank with Aadhaar + PAN + business name
   → Takes 2-3 days

2. Get MSME/Udyam Registration (optional but recommended)
   → Go to udyamregistration.gov.in
   → Free registration
   → Gives you credibility and access to government schemes

3. Get GST Registration (needed when revenue exceeds ₹20 lakhs/year)
   → Apply at gst.gov.in
   → Free registration
   → Takes 3-5 working days
   → Needed to collect and pass on GST to government

4. Open Razorpay / Payment Gateway Account
   → Needs current bank account + PAN + business proof
   → Razorpay onboarding takes 2-3 days
```

**Total cost: ₹2,000-5,000**
**Total time: 1-2 weeks**

---

### Step 3 — Register as Private Limited Company (When Scaling)

**Why Pvt Ltd:**
- Required for raising investment (angels, VCs)
- More credibility with enterprise clients
- Limited liability — personal assets protected
- Can issue shares to co-founders and employees

**Documents needed:**
- Aadhaar and PAN of all directors
- Address proof of registered office
- Digital Signature Certificate (DSC) for each director
- Director Identification Number (DIN)

**Process:**
```
1. Get DSC for each director
   → From certified agencies like eMudhra or Sify
   → Cost: ₹1,000-1,500 per director
   → Time: 2-3 days

2. Apply for company name approval
   → On MCA portal — mca.gov.in
   → Submit SPICe+ form
   → Cost: ₹1,000
   → Time: 2-3 days

3. File SPICe+ incorporation form
   → MCA portal
   → Includes PAN + TAN application automatically
   → Cost: ₹5,000-7,000 (government fees)
   → Time: 7-10 days

4. Receive Certificate of Incorporation
   → Company is officially registered
   → You get CIN (Corporate Identity Number)

5. Open company current account
   → With Certificate of Incorporation + PAN + Aadhaar

6. Register for GST
   → gst.gov.in with company PAN

7. Register for Shops and Establishment Act
   → State-specific requirement
   → Local municipal office or online portal
   → Cost: ₹1,000-2,000
```

**Total government cost: ₹10,000-15,000**
**Total time: 2-3 weeks**
**Recommended: Use a CA or LegalDesk.com to handle paperwork — costs ₹5,000 extra but saves your time**

### Step 4 — Additional Registrations Needed for This Platform

| Registration | When Needed | Where | Cost |
|---|---|---|---|
| GST Registration | Before first transaction | gst.gov.in | Free |
| Payment Aggregator license | When processing >₹10Cr/year | RBI | Complex — use Razorpay as aggregator instead |
| Startup India Registration | Anytime | startupindia.gov.in | Free — gives tax benefits |

### Step 5 — Important Compliance After Registration

| Compliance | Frequency | Cost |
|---|---|---|
| GST filing | Monthly | ₹2,000-3,000/month (CA fee) |
| TDS filing | Quarterly | Included with CA |
| Annual ROC filing (Pvt Ltd) | Annually | ₹5,000-10,000 |
| Income tax filing | Annually | ₹5,000-15,000 |
| Accounting / bookkeeping | Monthly | ₹3,000-5,000/month |

**Recommendation: Hire a CA from day one. Cost ₹5,000-8,000/month. Saves you from compliance headaches.**

## 4. Startup India Benefits (Don't Miss This)

Register at startupindia.gov.in for:
- **3 year income tax holiday** — pay zero tax for first 3 years
- **80% rebate on patent filing fees**
- **Fast track patent examination**
- **₹10 lakh seed fund** eligibility (SISFS scheme)
- **Government tender benefits** — easier to bid
- Free legal support for IP registration

**This is completely free and takes 2-3 days online. Do this before you start earning.**
